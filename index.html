Daily Presence

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2025

The short version: Daily Presence stores all your personal data locally on your device. We do not operate servers, do not collect your data, and do not share anything with third parties. Your journal is yours alone.

1. Who We Are

Daily Presence is a personal spirituality and mindfulness app. For questions about this policy, contact us at dailypresenceapp@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect and How It Is Stored

All data you enter in Daily Presence is stored exclusively on your device using an encrypted local database. We do not transmit your data to any server.

  • Account credentials — Your email address is stored as a one-way cryptographic hash. Your password is salted and hashed; the plaintext is never stored.
  • Journal entries — Your reflections, prompts, and mood logs are stored only on your device.
  • Practice tasks and streaks — Your spiritual practices, completion history, and daily intentions are stored locally.
  • Profile information — Your name, custom spiritual title, and profile photo are stored locally on your device.
  • Meditation sessions — Timer preferences and sound selections are stored locally.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently erased.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
  • We do not use advertising networks or sell data to any third party.
  • We do not have access to the content of your journal entries.

4. In-App Purchases

Daily Presence offers an optional one-time Premium upgrade. All payment transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive, store, or process your payment information. Purchase records are managed by the respective platform according to their own privacy policies.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera / Photo Library — Used only if you choose to set a profile photo. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Daily Presence is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Data Security

Your data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device. Passwords are protected using industry-standard salted hashing (SHA-256). Because all data remains on your device, its security also depends on your device's own security settings (screen lock, encryption).

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store your data on any server, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on our end. To delete your data, simply delete the app from your device. To export your journal, use the built-in CSV export feature (Premium).

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at dailypresenceapp@gmail.com.
